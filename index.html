Koduri Srisaila Sri Rajamouli born 10 October 1973 is an Indian filmmaker who primarily works in Telugu cinema. Known for his epic, action and fantasy genre films, he is considered the highest grossing Indian director of all time,[2] as well as the highest-paid director in Indian cinema.

James Francis Cameron CC (born August 16, 1954) is a Canadian filmmaker. He is a major figure in the post-New Hollywood era and often uses novel technologies with a classical filmmaking style. He first gained recognition for writing and directing The Terminator (1984).
